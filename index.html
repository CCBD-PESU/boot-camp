Greetings from CCBD!

We are back with Boot Camp 2021! The Center for Cloud Computing and Big Data of PES University is conducting an online Bootcamp for 4th semester CSE students who wish to work as summer interns. Attending the Bootcamp and completing the assignments that follow within the given deadline is mandatory for a Summer Internship opportunity at CCBD.

Once Registration is complete, the Centre will enable the link for Google Drive which consists of Study Material for BootCamp. Make sure to watch all the videos and solve one of the Problems shared with you. The solution must be uploaded to Google drive for which link will be shared later; any plagiarism detected will render all students ineligible to work at CCBD. Viva will be scheduled for submitted solutions.

Filling the given form should be done Individually and Solving the Problem Statement can be done in a group of 3 or 4 Students and Uploaded.
